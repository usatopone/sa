Accessibility notes

Public transport

Most major bus interchanges and ferries have step‑free access and priority seating. Verify lift outages on operator sites before travel.

Attractions

  • Ask for quiet rooms or sensory‑friendly times
  • Check step counts in heritage sites
  • Reserve wheelchairs where available
